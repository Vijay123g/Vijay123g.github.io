My Journey in Technology

From undergraduate studies to professional experience and graduate research

My Passion for Technology

My journey in computer science began during my undergraduate studies at CMR Technical Campus, where I developed a strong foundation in programming and software development. This passion led me to pursue professional opportunities at leading technology companies and eventually continue my education at Old Dominion University.

Professional Growth

During my time at Accenture, I gained valuable experience in full-stack development using the MEAN stack, working on enterprise-level applications. This experience solidified my interest in building scalable web applications and solving complex technical challenges.

Research Focus

I'm particularly interested in developing secure platforms for AI competitions and exploring the intersection of artificial intelligence and cybersecurity.

Technical Philosophy

I believe in writing clean, maintainable code and following best practices in software development. I'm passionate about learning new technologies and frameworks, and I enjoy the challenge of turning complex problems into elegant solutions.
